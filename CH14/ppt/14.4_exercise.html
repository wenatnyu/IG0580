Part 1: Review - Completing the Square Method

Steps to Complete the Square:

  1. Make sure the coefficient of \(x^2\) is 1
  2. Take half of the coefficient of \(x\), square it, and add/subtract it
  3. Write as \((x + p)^2 + q\) form
  4. Solve by taking square roots

Example: \(x^2 + 6x + 5 = 0\)

Step 1: \(x^2 + 6x = -5\)

Step 2: Half of 6 is 3, squared is 9. Add 9 to both sides.

Step 3: \(x^2 + 6x + 9 = -5 + 9\) ‚Üí \((x + 3)^2 = 4\)

Step 4: \(x + 3 = ¬±2\) ‚Üí \(x = -1\) or \(x = -5\)

Part 2: Practice Exercises

Ex 1. Complete the square for \(x^2 + 10x - 3 = 0\) and solve.
\(x^2 + 10x = 3\)
Half of 10 is 5, squared is 25.
\((x + 5)^2 = 3 + 25 = 28\)
\(x + 5 = ¬±\sqrt{28} = ¬±2\sqrt{7}\)
\(x = -5 ¬± 2\sqrt{7}\)
Ex 2. Complete the square: \(x^2 - 4x + 1 = 0\)
\(x^2 - 4x = -1\)
Half of -4 is -2, squared is 4.
\((x - 2)^2 = -1 + 4 = 3\)
\(x = 2 ¬± \sqrt{3}\)
Ex 3. Solve \(x^2 + 8x = 0\) by completing the square.
Half of 8 is 4, squared is 16.
\((x + 4)^2 = 0 + 16 = 16\)
\(x + 4 = ¬±4\)
\(x = 0\) or \(x = -8\)
Ex 4. Solve \(x^2 - 12x + 11 = 0\)
\(x^2 - 12x = -11\)
Half of -12 is -6, squared is 36.
\((x - 6)^2 = -11 + 36 = 25\)
\(x - 6 = ¬±5\)
\(x = 11\) or \(x = 1\)

Part 3: Homework Practice

Q1. Complete the square and solve: \(x^2 + 14x - 15 = 0\)
\(x^2 + 14x = 15\)
\((x + 7)^2 = 15 + 49 = 64\)
\(x = -7 ¬± 8\)
\(x = 1\) or \(x = -15\)
Q2. Solve \(x^2 - 2x + 5 = 0\) by completing the square.
\(x^2 - 2x = -5\)
\((x - 1)^2 = -5 + 1 = -4\)
No real solutions (negative under square root)
Q3. Complete the square: \(x^2 + 4x + 3 = 0\)
\(x^2 + 4x = -3\)
\((x + 2)^2 = -3 + 4 = 1\)
\(x + 2 = ¬±1\)
\(x = -1\) or \(x = -3\)
Q4. Solve \(x^2 - 16x = 0\)
\((x - 8)^2 = 0 + 64 = 64\)
\(x = 8 ¬± 8\)
\(x = 0\) or \(x = 16\)
Q5. (Application) The depth \(h\) of a pond is given by \(h = 3x^2 + 12x - 2\). Find the depth when \(x = -2\) hours.
\(h = 3(-2)^2 + 12(-2) - 2\)
\(h = 3(4) - 24 - 2\)
\(h = 12 - 24 - 2 = -14\) meters
