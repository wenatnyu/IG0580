Mathematical Vocabulary Dictation

Part 1: Concept Completion

Read the definition and write the English term.

Two or more linear equations that are solved at the same time are called Simultaneous equations.
In the algebraic fraction \( \frac{a}{b} \), the top part \( a \) is the numerator, and the bottom part \( b \) is the Denominator.
The formula \( x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \) is known as the Quadratic formula.
The longest side of a right-angled triangle, opposite the right angle, is called the Hypotenuse.
Bearings are always measured in a Clockwise direction from the North line.
The method of changing \( x^2 + 6x \) into the form \( (x+3)^2 - 9 \) is called Completing the square.
A drawing where 1 cm represents 1 km in real life is called a Scale drawing.
