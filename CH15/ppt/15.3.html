Part 1: Key Concepts (SOH CAH TOA)

Right-Angled Triangles Only:

  • \( \sin \theta = \frac{\text{Opposite}}{\text{Hypotenuse}} \)
  • \( \cos \theta = \frac{\text{Adjacent}}{\text{Hypotenuse}} \)
  • \( \tan \theta = \frac{\text{Opposite}}{\text{Adjacent}} \)

Finding a Side: Use algebra (multiply or swap).
Finding an Angle: Use inverse functions (\( \sin^{-1}, \cos^{-1}, \tan^{-1} \)).

Part 2: In-Class Examples

Ex 1. (Find Side) Hypotenuse = 12 cm, Angle = \( 35^\circ \). Find the Opposite side.
\( \sin 35 = \frac{x}{12} \Rightarrow x = 12 \times \sin 35 \approx 6.88 \).
Ex 2. (Find Angle) Opposite = 8 cm, Adjacent = 6 cm. Find angle \( \theta \).
\( \tan \theta = \frac{8}{6} \Rightarrow \theta = \tan^{-1}(1.333) \approx 53.1^\circ \).

Part 3: Homework Practice
Chapter 15.3
Q1. (Find Hypotenuse) A right-angled triangle has an angle of \( 40^\circ \) and an adjacent side of 10 cm. Calculate the length of the hypotenuse.
\( \cos 40 = \frac{10}{H} \Rightarrow H = \frac{10}{\cos 40} \approx 13.1 \text{ cm} \).
Q2. (Ladder Problem) A ladder 8m long rests against a vertical wall. The base of the ladder is 2.5m from the wall. Calculate the angle the ladder makes with the ground.
Adj = 2.5, Hyp = 8. Use Cos.
\( \cos x = \frac{2.5}{8} \Rightarrow x = \cos^{-1}(0.3125) \approx 71.8^\circ \).
Q3. (Rectangle) A rectangle measures 15 cm by 8 cm. Calculate the length of the diagonal and the angle the diagonal makes with the longer side.
Diagonal (Pythagoras): \( \sqrt{15^2+8^2} = 17 \text{ cm} \).
Angle (tan): \( \tan x = \frac{8}{15} \Rightarrow x \approx 28.1^\circ \).
Q4. (Elevation) A tree casts a shadow 20m long when the sun's angle of elevation is \( 55^\circ \). Calculate the height of the tree.
Adj = 20, Angle = 55. Find Opp.
\( \tan 55 = \frac{h}{20} \Rightarrow h = 20 \times \tan 55 \approx 28.6 \text{ m} \).
Q5. (Harder Algebra) In a triangle, \( \sin x = 0.6 \). If the Hypotenuse is 15 cm, find the length of the Opposite side without using a calculator for the angle.
\( \sin x = \frac{Opp}{Hyp} \Rightarrow 0.6 = \frac{Opp}{15} \).
\( Opp = 0.6 \times 15 = 9 \text{ cm} \).
