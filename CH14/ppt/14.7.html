14.7 Algebraic Fractions / 代数分式

1. Intro Problems (10 minutes) / 引入问题（10 分钟）

Objective / 目标： To introduce algebraic fractions, remind students about simplifying numeric fractions, and link the idea of “common factor” and “common denominator” to algebraic expressions.
介绍代数分式，复习分数化简，并将“公因式、公分母”的概念延伸到代数式。

Problem 1: From numeric to algebraic / 从数字分数到代数分式

Simplify the fractions. / 化简下列分数：

  1. \(\dfrac{12}{18}\)
  2. \(\dfrac{15}{35}\)

Ask: “What did you do?” (Divide by a common factor.)
提问：“你做了什么操作？”（用公因数约分。）

Problem 2: First algebraic fraction / 第一个代数分式

Now simplify the algebraic fraction. / 现在化简代数分式：

\(\dfrac{6x}{9x}\)
  • What can we cancel? / 可以约去什么？
  • What must we say about \(x\)? / 关于 \(x\) 需要说明什么？

Emphasise: denominator cannot be zero, so here \(x \neq 0\).
强调：分母不能为 0，所以此处 \(x \neq 0\)。

Problem 3: Algebraic fraction with factorising / 带因式分解的代数分式

Consider: / 思考：

\(\dfrac{x^{2} - 9}{x^{2} - 3x}\)

Guide steps / 引导步骤：

  1. Factorise numerator and denominator. / 对分子、分母分别因式分解。
  2. Cancel common factors. / 约去公因式。
  3. State restrictions on \(x\). / 写出 \(x\) 的取值限制。

This connects 14.6 factorising with algebraic fractions.
这一步把 14.6 的因式分解和本节代数分式联系起来。

2. In-Class Practice (20 minutes) / 课堂练习（20 分钟）

Objective / 目标： To practise simplifying, adding, subtracting, multiplying and dividing simple algebraic fractions.
练习代数分式的化简、加减乘除。

Practice 1: Simplifying algebraic fractions / 化简代数分式

Simplify each expression and state any restrictions on the variable. / 化简并写出变量的限制条件：

  1. \(\dfrac{4x}{10x}\)
  2. \(\dfrac{x^{2} - 16}{x^{2} - 4x}\)
  3. \(\dfrac{3x^{2} + 6x}{9x}\)

Practice 2: Adding and subtracting / 分式的加减

Perform the operations and simplify your answers. / 进行加减运算，并化简结果：

  1. \(\dfrac{2}{x} + \dfrac{3}{x}\)
  2. \(\dfrac{1}{x} + \dfrac{2}{x+1}\)
  3. \(\dfrac{3}{x-2} - \dfrac{1}{x-2}\)

Practice 3: Multiplying and dividing / 分式的乘除

Multiply or divide and simplify. / 进行乘除运算并化简：

  1. \(\dfrac{2x}{3} \times \dfrac{9}{x}\)
  2. \(\dfrac{x+1}{2x} \div \dfrac{x+1}{4}\)

Tips / 提示： Before adding or subtracting, use a common denominator. For multiplying and dividing, factorise and cancel before multiplying.
加减前先找公分母；乘除时先因式分解、约分，再进行运算。
