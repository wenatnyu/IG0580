    [6]
    Answer & working
    Equations:
    ① \( 2x + 3y = 17 \)
    ② \( x − y = 4 \Rightarrow x = y + 4 \).

    Substitute into ①:
    \( 2(y + 4) + 3y = 17 \)
    \( 2y + 8 + 3y = 17 \Rightarrow 5y + 8 = 17 \Rightarrow 5y = 9 \Rightarrow y = \dfrac{9}{5} \).

    Then \( x = y + 4 = \dfrac{9}{5} + \dfrac{20}{5} = \dfrac{29}{5} \).

    Answers:
    \( x = \dfrac{29}{5} \) (or 5.8),   \( y = \dfrac{9}{5} \) (or 1.8)

    Quadratic Equations – Factorising – Answers
    [6]
    Answer & working
    Expression: \( x^2 − 7x + 10 \).

    (a) Factorise:
    Find two numbers whose product is 10 and sum is 7 → 5 and 2.
    So \( x^2 − 7x + 10 = (x − 5)(x − 2) \).

    (b) Solve \( x^2 − 7x + 10 = 0 \):
    \( (x − 5)(x − 2) = 0 \Rightarrow x = 5 \) or \( x = 2 \).

    (c) When \( x = 3 \):
    \( 3^2 − 7×3 + 10 = 9 − 21 + 10 = -2 \).

    Answers:
    (a) \( (x − 5)(x − 2) \)
    (b) \( x = 5 \) or \( x = 2 \)
    (c) −2

    Algebraic Fractions – Answers
    [6]
    Answer & working
    (a) Simplify \( 3x / 9x^2 \):
    \( \dfrac{3x}{9x^2} = \dfrac{3x}{9x^2} = \dfrac{1}{3x} \).
    This can be written in the form \( k/x \) as \( \dfrac{1/3}{x} \).

    Answer: \( \dfrac{1}{3x} \) (or \( \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \dfrac{1}{x} \)).

    (b) Solve \( (x − 1)/3 = (2x + 5)/6 \):
    Multiply both sides by 6:
    \( 2(x − 1) = 2x + 5 \)
    \( 2x − 2 = 2x + 5 \Rightarrow -2 = 5 \), which is impossible.

    So there is no solution to this equation.

    Answers:
    (a) \( \dfrac{1}{3x} \)
    (b) No solution

    Sequences (Quadratic) – Answers
    [7]
    Answer & working
    Sequence: 3, 8, 15, 24, 35, ...

    First differences: 5, 7, 9, 11
    Second differences: 2, 2, 2 (constant) → quadratic sequence.

    (a) Next two terms
    Next first differences: 13, then 15.
    So
      T6 = 35 + 13 = 48
      T7 = 48 + 15 = 63.

    (b) Find the nth term Tn
    Assume Tn = an² + bn + c. Use n = 1, 2, 3:
    n = 1: a + b + c = 3 … (1)
    n = 2: 4a + 2b + c = 8 … (2)
    n = 3: 9a + 3b + c = 15 … (3)

    (2) − (1): 3a + b = 5 … (A)
    (3) − (2): 5a + b = 7 … (B)
    (B) − (A): 2a = 2 ⇒ a = 1.
    Then 3a + b = 5 ⇒ 3 + b = 5 ⇒ b = 2.
    From (1): a + b + c = 3 ⇒ 1 + 2 + c = 3 ⇒ c = 0.

    所以：Tn = n² + 2n.

    (c) For which value of n is Tn = 120?
    解方程 n² + 2n = 120：
    n² + 2n − 120 = 0.
    因式分解：(n + 12)(n − 10) = 0.
    n = 10 或 n = −12，舍去负数解。

    Answer: n = 10.
